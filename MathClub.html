Math Club

Viking Academics

Maths Club helps in arousing and maintaining students’ interest in mathematics. It provides the students with opportunities to develop their explorative, creative and inventive skills. It offers an informal and a social atmosphere where all can frilly ask questions related to the material taught in class, discuss with the teacher or peers problems outside of the syllabus, exchange mathematical ideas and provide a helpful criticism of these ideas. This is also the time when the MS and HS students get ready for the maths contests organized by University of Waterloo.
